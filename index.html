World Color Survey

110 UNWRITTEN LANGUAGES, EACH 24 NATIVE SPEAKERS

WCS was initiated in the late 1970s to investigate how native speakers around the world classify colors. 24 members of 110 societies with unwritten languages were asked to name 330 Munsell color chips. Explore the results below!
